## Limitations of EDR Tools

EDR solutions excel at identifying malicious activity on endpoints. However, they typically fall short when it comes to monitoring identity-related threats. EDR tools are designed to catch malware, ransomware, and other endpoint-based attacks but leave many identity threats unmonitored. Taking a broader view, identity threats can manifest in myriad ways that EDR solutions aren't equipped to track.
